Our Mission

At Vienna High School, Kabowa, our mission is to provide a well-rounded education that empowers students to excel academically, socially, and personally. We believe in the power of hard work and discipline to unlock the full potential of every student.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and a nurturing environment where students grow into responsible and successful global citizens.

Our History

Vienna High School was established in 2005 in Kabowa, Kampala. From a small beginning with only 50 students, we have grown to accommodate over 1,000 students. Our commitment to quality education and values has made us a reputable institution in Uganda.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in all aspects of education.
  • Integrity: Building trust through honesty and fairness.
  • Discipline: Encouraging responsibility and self-control.
  • Creativity: Nurturing innovation and problem-solving skills.

Our Facilities

We provide modern facilities to enhance the learning experience:

  • Well-equipped science and computer labs
  • A vast library with a wide range of resources
  • Sports grounds for football, basketball, and athletics
  • Hostel accommodation for both boys and girls
